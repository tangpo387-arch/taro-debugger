## 3. Edge Cases & Design Decisions

- **Modularity**: Split the monolithic `LogViewerComponent` into two dedicated viewports (`DebugConsole` and `OutputConsole`) to isolate evaluation logic from program output and support future protocol inspection.
- **Service Relocation**: `DapLogService` was moved to the library as it is the SSOT for all console streams and is required by the library components.
- **Visual Design**: Re-implemented tab styling to match the "Surface Fusion" and "Flush IDE" metaphors (32px headers, 1px dividers, custom background colors for active states).
- **Sass Refactor**: Migrated from `@import` to `@use` to align with modern Sass standards and suppress deprecation warnings during building.
- 🔍 **Inspect**: `DebugConsoleComponent.scrollToBottom()` and its interaction with `onTabChange()`. Verify that the 50ms timeout is sufficient for `cdk-virtual-scroll-viewport` to remeasure after a tab visibility change.
