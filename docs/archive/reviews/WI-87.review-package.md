## 3. Edge Cases & Design Decisions

- **Decision**: Merged `terminated` into `idle` to fix the "stuck transport" bug where server-initiated termination didn't trigger `reset()`.
- **Concurrency**: Introduced a private `isDisconnecting` boolean flag in `DapSessionService` to prevent race conditions during manual `disconnect()` calls, as we can no longer rely on the public state being `terminated` during the transition.
- 🔍 **Inspect**: `DapSessionService.disconnect()` finally block — verified that `isDisconnecting` is always reset and `reset()` is always called.
