## 3. Edge Cases & Design Decisions

- Decision: Used two reactive effects—one to synchronize `viewAnchor` with `currentPc` inputs, and one to trigger `relocateWindow`—keeping state management cleanly separated from execution updates.
- 🔍 Inspect: The `updateInstructions` and `ResizeObserver` callbacks in `assembly-view.component.ts` — verify they now correctly read from `this.viewAnchor()` instead of `this.currentPc()`.
