## 3. Edge Cases & Design Decisions

- **Production Simplification & Test Cleanup**: By ensuring the custom mock transport factory returned from `TransportFactoryService` in test setups is automatically injected into the `DapSessionService` constructor, we completely eliminated the need for a complex getter/setter property bridge in production.
- **Redundant Override Elimination**: Removed 15 redundant occurrences of `(service as any).transport = mockTransport;` across `dap-session.service.spec.ts` and `connection-error-integration.spec.ts`. The production code now utilizes a clean, standard, private `transport` field, while keeping tests completely de-cluttered and 100% green.
- **Event and Traffic Forwarding**: The `DapRequestBroker` exposes reactive subjects for internal errors/warnings and diagnostic traffic. `DapSessionService` subscribes to these streams on construction and forwards them to its public observables, preserving all business pipelines intact.
