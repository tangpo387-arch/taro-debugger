## 3. Edge Cases & Design Decisions

- **Address 64-bit Formatting**: Designed address calculations to map accurately onto absolute target references, explicitly padding with `padStart(16, '0')` to support absolute 64-bit coordinates space.
- **Rollback Consistency**: On failed write triggers (e.g. read-only segments or GDB error), the byte value reverts immediately to its pre-edit value, and the ASCII row preview is fully re-parsed from the reverted state.
- 🔍 **Inspect**: The `confirmEdit` method in `memory-view.component.ts` — verify that cell inputs are instantly closed and cleaned up before the DAP write round-trip completes to prevent double-confirm locks.
