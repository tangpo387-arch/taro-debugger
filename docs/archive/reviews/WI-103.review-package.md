## 3. Edge Cases & Design Decisions

- **Optimistic UI**: Clicks in the gutter immediately update the SSOT in `DapSessionService`, which broadcasts to the Editor. This ensures zero-latency visual feedback for breakpoint toggles.
- **Protocol Handshake**: Added `resyncAllBreakpointsInternal` to `DapSessionService.startSession()`. This ensures breakpoints are sent *before* `configurationDone`, fixing a potential race condition where breakpoints were sent too late in the previous implementation.
- **SSOT Reactivity**: The Editor now uses `takeUntilDestroyed` and subscribes directly to `breakpoints$`. It rebuilds its decorations only when the stream emits, ensuring complete synchronization with other UI parts (like the Breakpoints Panel).
